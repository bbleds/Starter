OUR VISION

Our vision is to be a church of spirit-filled people, standing ready to serve our community and
lead others to know the fullness of the gospel of Jesus Christ. We want to guide others on their
journey to meeting God, whatever path of life they may be following. We want to continue
planting churches around our town and the Middle Tennessee area and see our communities
transformed by the power of Christ and the shared love of Him and His church.

MORE ABOUT OUR VISION
HIS KINGDOM

At TPOM, we believe whole-heartedly that every person has a responsibility to the Kingdom of
God. That duty manifests itself differently for everyone and that's the beautiful thing about
ministry: it's all unique and it's all important! Whether helping with our children's classes or
singing with a worship team in our main services, TPOM believes in the power of individuals
equipped in ministry and we love to see the fruit of this dedication.

YOUR CONNECTION

Have you ever wanted to be a part of something bigger than yourself? At TPOM, you have the
ability to plug in and truly make a difference. It is more than just sitting on a pew, it is about
making a connection. Experience a life made new through the power and redeeming love of
Jesus Christ. Come grow with us and create a lasting impact on the world around you.
